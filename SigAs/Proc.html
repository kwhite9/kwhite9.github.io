Instructions
For each step you want to add, press the associated button. You will be prompted with certain questions depending on what button you press.
For example, if the first step of your procedure is to add ethanol to an erlenmeyer flask, press "add component".
You will be asked what you would like to add (ethanol) and what you would like to add it to (an erlenmeyer flask).
Once you've entered that in, The first step under "procedure" will change to "1. add ethanol to erlenmeyer flask"
Continue doing this for all of the steps of your procedure. There are buttons for adding a component, stirring, refluxing, washing, and drying.
Finally, some reactions need to be done in a completely water-free environment, so they can't be exposed to air (or water vapor will get in).
If this is the case, check the box at the bottom labelled "This reaction needs to be water/air-free".
This will add a reminder to do everything under a nitrogen atmosphere at the top of your procedure.
